Welcome to Find Ten Seconds

The aim of the game is to make pairs of similar tiles. You select a tile and then find a second one that match as closely as possible. Each round you have to find ten pairs before the board replenish

How to play

  1. 1. Select a first tile: Pick a tile from the board by clicking it. It will appear selected on the right. Click it again to deselect it
  2. 2. Find a second tile: Hover on another tile from the board to see how many points the pair will get you
    • - An exact match of symbol (same letter, or same number) will get you the most
    • - A match of color will get you points too
    • - If you can't find an exact match, you can make a pair of vowels, or odd number. Try to find what brings the most point
  3. Click on the tile to confirm the selection
  4. 3. Repeat until you find ten pairs
  5. 4. When you have found 10 pairs: the board will replenish. You get a new tile for every 20 points you got this round.
  6. 5. The game end when there is no more tiles
Every Ten Seconds a tile disappears from the board
You can configure board size, toggle easy mode (tiles don't disappear), restart the game or mute the music from the controls on the top right
